Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What parks are included in the app?
A: Park Explorer NC includes all North Carolina State Parks. Park data is sourced from publicly available information and government resources.
Q: How often is park information updated?
A: Park data is updated periodically, but may not reflect the most current information. Always verify critical details like operating hours, fees, and trail conditions with official park sources before visiting.
Q: Can I use the app offline?
A: Basic park information can be accessed offline after it has been loaded. However, maps and directions require an active internet connection.
Q: Why do I need to accept the disclaimer?
A: The disclaimer outlines the terms of use and reminds users that outdoor activities involve inherent risks. It's important that users understand they are responsible for their own safety when visiting parks.
Q: Is my location data shared or stored?
A: Location data is only used locally on your device to help you find nearby parks and get directions. We do not collect, store, or share your location information.
Q: How do I get directions to a park?
A: Tap on any park to view its details, then use the "Get Directions" button to open directions in Apple Maps.
